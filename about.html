Ancient Egypt

While conducting my research I learnt that people argue about who represents Pharoah in the bible, because he was never named. Meaning that there is a high possibility that Ramses is not presented in the bible. When the generations were throned, they were called Pharaohs, they were never called by their names. The only Rameses we are aware of in the bible was the land or city in Egypt. In the bible we learn about two generations of Pharaohs, the father and the son. It is a beautiful story yet a tragic one, the first generation (first Pharaoh) feared that the Israelites might take over Egypt if they populate, therefore he made an unpredictable move and demanded that the first-born sons of the Israelites that were below the age of three to be killed. This way he was able to instill fear in the Israelites and made it easy for him to enslave them and have control over them
